How it works

From webcam to insight,
in real time

No installation beyond the extension. No account required. No data ever leaves your browser.

01
Webcam captures you
Your existing laptop webcam is used. No special hardware, no extra setup. Camera permission is requested once.
02
AI maps your skeleton
MediaPipe Pose runs in-browser via WebAssembly, detecting 33 body landmarks at 30 frames per second. Zero cloud.
03
Angles are calculated
Neck tilt, shoulder alignment, and spine curvature are computed every frame using precise trigonometric angle math.
04
You're alerted precisely
A specific alert fires after 90 seconds of sustained bad posture — not a generic reminder, but the exact metric that broke.
Features

Built for people who
actually work all day

Every decision was made for the person who can't afford to stop and think about their posture — so PostureAI thinks for them.

Real-time posture scoring

A single live 0–100 score computed every frame from three weighted metrics: neck tilt (35%), shoulder alignment (30%), and spine curvature (35%). The score updates as you move, giving you instant feedback rather than post-session summaries.

30 FPS · No lag

Complete privacy

MediaPipe runs via WebAssembly in your browser tab. No server, no API, no video upload. Architecturally impossible to leak — not just a policy.

Zero upload

Precise alerts

Alerts name the exact problem: "Chin tuck — your head is too far forward." Not a generic nudge. Fires at 90 seconds, not 8 minutes.

Specific · Fast

Session history

Your posture scores are stored in IndexedDB locally. 7-day trend charts show whether you're improving. No account required.

Works anywhere

A Chrome extension that floats a score widget on any tab, silently tracking while you work, browse, or write.
